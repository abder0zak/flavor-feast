Prep

15 Min

Cook

20 Min

Serves

Garden Fresh Buddha Bowl

"A nutrient-dense bowl that brings the garden to your table."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Organic Quinoa
  • 2 cups Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 Avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Tahini Dressing

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Boil quinoa in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes.
  3. Massage the spinach with a dash of olive oil and salt.
  4. Assemble the bowl starting with a base of quinoa.
